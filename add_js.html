About Me

I am a Doctoral Lecturer in the Computer Science Department at Hunter College, New York. My research focuses on Machine Learning, particularly Deep Learning and Large Language Models. With a PhD in Machine Learning, I am passionate about bridging theoretical foundations with practical applications in artificial intelligence.
